Description

The Enterprise Optimization Roadmap view is used to assist consultants who are leading or participating in this core Oracle Business Consulting service offering. This offering is designed to help organizations maximize the value of their Oracle investment and establish a clear focus on business results throughout the technology lifecycle. The view provides access to a predefined set of OUM material appropriate for this type of engagement.
