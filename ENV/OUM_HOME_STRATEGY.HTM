VIEW DESCRIPTION AND KEY COMPONENTS

Description

The Strategy view provides access to the tasks used to form a strategy or approach to technical IT domains in the enterprise as a whole. This view is based only on the tasks within the Envision focus area. It allows the enterprise to explore technical approaches that could be utilized to resolve specific business problems. The Strategy view may be used to develop an approach around Business Intelligence, Service-Oriented Architecture, Security and many other domains. This Strategy view is generic. Used as a foundation, these tasks accomplish initial discovery and requirements gathering.
