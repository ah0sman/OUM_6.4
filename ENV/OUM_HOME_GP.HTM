VIEW DESCRIPTION AND KEY COMPONENTS

Description

The SOA Governance Planning view is used to help organizations build a SOA Governance Model that manages the SOA key assets. Many enterprises struggle with SOA adoption, and one of the reasons is that the benefits of SOA do not come automatically. A company needs an appropriate SOA Governance Model that manages the SOA key assets such as Services, Reference Architecture and the Service Infrastructure Layer. Performing a planning engagement based on this view provides a roadmap for the adoption of SOA Governance tailored to the manner in which a given organization works. During the engagement, you work with the customer to capture relevant information about Governance structures and practices, facilitate the development of the Governance vision and work with the customer’s staff to develop the Governance Roadmap. As with all SOA planning views, this view is used to plan one particular aspect of the SOA strategy and can be combined with other SOA planning views based on the organization’s needs.
