BPM in OUM Overview

Business Process Management (BPM) in OUM consists of the following:

For engagements with a program scope (enterprise-level), OUM supports the following:

  • Roadmap Creation, which provides the guidance to successfully adopt a business process oriented approach to building IT solutions to support the business

For engagements with a project scope, OUM supports the following:

  • BPM Project Engineering, which provides a framework to effectively deliver composite business application projects in a BPM environment.

BPM Roadmap

Description

The Business Process Management (BPM) Roadmap view is based on the BPM Roadmap described in the IT Strategies from Oracle (ITSO) guidelines and reference architectures library, Creating a BPM Roadmap. A BPM Roadmap provides the guidance to successfully adopt a business process oriented approach to building IT solutions to support the business.

BPM Project Engineering

Description

The Business Process Management (BPM) Project Engineering view is based on the IT Strategies from Oracle (ITSO) Business Process Engineering Practitioner Guide. The Oracle Business Process Engineering framework introduces a discipline to effectively deliver composite business application projects in a BPM environment.
