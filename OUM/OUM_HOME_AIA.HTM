Description

The SOA Application Integration Architecture (AIA) Project Delivery view is used to execute SOA implementations that utilize AIA. Oracle Application Integration Architecture provides an open standards based framework for creating cross-application business processes that support the way you run your business today, while paving the way for your long term, strategic, business transformation plans. Its application independent framework enables you to utilize the applications of your choice to create composite business processes unique to your business, on a flexible service-oriented architecture.
