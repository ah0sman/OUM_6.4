The following table provides an index of tasks within OUM where additional tools or technical guidelines are available.  These guidelines are not meant as tool recommendations, but as references to where a particular tool may be applied within OUM, if it is the tool of choice for your project. PLEASE NOTE:
  • The links to these guidelines may also be obtained by accessing the task guidance for an individual task.
  • There are many tools available to accomplish development of OUM Artifacts, your project may have chosen a tool not listed here.
  • Task level tool guidance does not exist for ALL OUM tasks where a tool may be applied.
  • In some cases, references to material outside the method may be listed but not linked, you may copy the URL into your browser window if you have internet connectivity.
