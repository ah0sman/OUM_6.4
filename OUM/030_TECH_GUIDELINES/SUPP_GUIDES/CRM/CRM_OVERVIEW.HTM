SIEBEL CRM OVERVIEW

What is Oracle Siebel CRM?

Oracle's Siebel Customer Relationship Management (CRM) products cover the breadth of CRM functionality, from sales, marketing and e-commerce, to customer service, multi-channel interaction and analytics.

The following Oracle products are included within Siebel CRM:

  • Sales
  • Marketing and Loyalty
  • Contact Center and Service
  • Self-Service and E-Billing
  • Contact Center Infrastructure
  • Oracle CRM on Demand
  • Partner Relationship Management
  • Quote and Order Capture
  • Price Management
  • CRM Technology
  • Social CRM
  • CRM Gadgets

ORACLE SIEBEL CRM AND OUM

As Siebel CRM is a packaged application, the following additional OUM Supplemental Guide should also be considered when conducting a Siebel Implementation project.

  • Application Implementation Supplemental Guide

To assist practitioners in understanding how to employ OUM on a Siebel CRM project, the following content is provided to extend and complement the standard task and supplemental guidance indicated above. The Oracle Siebel CRM Supplemental Guide provides the following materials and tools to optimize the use of OUM.

  • Task mapping between OUM and the previous methodology used for Siebel implementations (i.e., Siebel Results Roadmap) reflecting the set of recommended and optional tasks required for a Siebel project)
  • Siebel-specific content for tasks that require more detail for Siebel CRM implementation projects

Which Siebel Product Set Does this Guide Address?

This supplemental guide addresses Siebel CRM On Premises only. Oracle CRM On Demand, CRM Technology products, Social CRM and migration projects are out of scope for this version of the guide.
