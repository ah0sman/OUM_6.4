ORACLE SUPPORT SERVICES SUPPLEMENTAL GUIDE

The materials presented in this supplemental guide provide specific information for OUM practitioners to provide the guidance needed to effectively manage and support the lifecycle of Oracle environments during an implementation and after go-live. The Oracle Support Services Supplemental Guide contains the following components:

Oracle Support Services Lifecycle Management Strategy Overview

This component provides an overview of Oracle Support Services lifecycle management strategy along with an overview of the Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) Service Lifecycle upon which it is based. ITIL® is a Registered Trade Mark of the Office of Government Commerce in the United Kingdom and other countries.

Oracle Support Services Lifecycle Management Methodology Mapping

This component provides a mapping between the OUM and ITIL lifecycle management methodologies. The methodology mapping should be used to gain an understanding of the relationship between OUM and ITIL, as well as how to leverage the value of the ITIL best practices to achieve excellence in the lifecycle management of any Oracle investment.

Oracle Support Services Supplemental Task Guidelines for Lifecycle Management of the My Oracle Support Services Portal

This component should be used in conjunction with the standard OUM task guidelines to supplement baseline guidance for affected tasks when planning and implementing the processes, policies and procedures used for lifecycle management of the My Oracle Support Services portal.

Oracle Support Services Supplemental Task Guidelines for IT Change Management

This component should be used in conjunction with the standard OUM task guidelines to supplement baseline guidance for affected tasks when planning and implementing the processes, policies and procedures used for IT Change Management in Oracle environments.

Oracle Support Services Supplemental Task Guidelines for Incident Management

This component should be used in conjunction with the standard OUM task guidelines to supplement baseline guidance for affected tasks when planning and implementing the processes, policies and procedures used for product support of your deployed applications. It focuses on Incident Management goals, processes and integration with your other IT service operations.

PURPOSE

The purpose of this document is to provide specific information for OUM practitioners regarding how to prepare customers to adopt and adapt established best practice guidance for effectively managing and supporting the lifecycle of Oracle environments during an implementation and after go-live.

This supplemental guide has been expanded for OUM 5.6 to include IT change management best practices in an Oracle Environment, as well as best practices contributing to the lifecycle management of the My Oracle Support Services portal.

My Oracle Support is an integral part of Oracle Customer Services’ ongoing commitment to customer and partner success. The My Oracle Support application platform uses personalized, proactive, and collaborative capabilities to manage the lifecycle of support, including access to knowledge base articles, proactive and preventive services, and automated support tools.

Change management is a key process for keeping systems healthy. This enhanced version of the supplemental guide provides Oracle best practices related to people, process and technology in order to facilitate efficient and effective management of IT changes.
