Process Classification Framework (PCF). PCF was developed by APQC and member companies as an open standard to facilitate improvement through process management and benchmarking regardless of industry, size, or geography. The PCF organizes operating and management processes into 12 enterprise-level categories, including process groups and over 1,500 processes and associated activities. The PCF and associated measures and benchmarking surveys are available for download and completion at no charge at
