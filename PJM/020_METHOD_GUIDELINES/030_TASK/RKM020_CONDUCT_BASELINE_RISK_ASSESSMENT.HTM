RKM.020 - CONDUCT BASELINE RISK ASSESSMENT

The purpose of this task is to identify risks early in Project Start Up, calculate their probability of occurrence and potential impact on the project, and develop options for handling each risk should it materialize during the course of the project.

ACTIVITY

PS.ACT.RBC Review Bid and Contract

Back to Top

WORK PRODUCT

Baseline Risk Assessment

This work product consists of a Risk Log that includes all the risks identified during the assessment. It provides a point-in-time snapshot of the risk at Project Start Up.

APPROACH

Identify Risks

Use the bid materials as a source to identify potential risks. Specifically, look at any Risk Analysis documents.

Once you have identified the risks, add them to your project Risk Log. In addition to a clear, concise description, include possible consequences as part of the description.

Analyze Each Risk

Analyze each risk for:

  • Criticality - Importance to the Success of the Project (3=High, 2=Medium, 1=Low)
  • Probability - Likelihood of Occurring (3=High, 2=Medium, 1=Low, 0=Closed)
  • Timeframe - Timeframe of the Potential Impact (3=Near, 2=Medium, 1=Far)
  • Severity - Criticality * Probability * Timeframe

Update the Risk Log with the analysis information.

Create a Mitigation Plan for Each Risk

For each risk, create a Mitigation Plan. The Mitigation Plan details a mitigating response for each trigger event. If possible, document that plan directly in the Risk Log. If the Mitigation Plan for a risk requires more detail than can be reasonably documented in the Risk Log, create a separate document.
