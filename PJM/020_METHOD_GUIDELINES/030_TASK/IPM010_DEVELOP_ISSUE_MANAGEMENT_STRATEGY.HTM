IPM.010 - DEVELOP ISSUE MANAGEMENT STRATEGY

The purpose of this task is to develop and document the Issue Management Strategy. The Issue Management Strategy establishes the procedures for managing and monitoring issues during the project.

ACTIVITY

PS.ACT.EG Establish Governance

Back to Top

WORK PRODUCT

Issue Management Strategy

The Issue Management Strategy is a component of the Project Management Plan (PMP) work product and documents the project procedures for managing and monitoring issues.

APPROACH

Determine Issue Management Steps

Identify all the steps for managing issues during the project. Consider the following example:

  1. Identify an issue.
  2. Add issue to Issue Log.
  3. Review issue and assign to responsible person.
  4. Assignee resolves issue, closes issue and updates Issue Log.
  5. Assignee cannot resolve issue and issue goes to Escalation Process.

Determine the Role(s) for Each Issue Management Step

Once you have identified all the steps, determine what role(s) is responsible for each step.

Create Issue Management Flow Diagram

Optionally, create a flow diagram showing the steps and the roles.

Provide Reporting Details

Provide details of how often you will report on issues, in what format, and to whom. This information should be consistent with information in the Project Team Communication Plan component of the PMP.
