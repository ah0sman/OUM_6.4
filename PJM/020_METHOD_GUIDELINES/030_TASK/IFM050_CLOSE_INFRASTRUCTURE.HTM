IFM.050 - CLOSE INFRASTRUCTURE

The purpose of this task is to close out the project infrastructure once the project is complete. This includes the following responsibilities:

  • Perform final backup and archive of technical environment.
  • Remove all confidential information from customer environment.
  • Turn over and document technical environment to customer.
  • Revoke all access to customer systems from project team members.
  • Inventory and return any customer owned assets, including software, supplies, office equipment, etc.
