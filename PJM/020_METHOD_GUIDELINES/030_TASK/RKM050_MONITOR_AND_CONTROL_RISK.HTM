RKM.050 - MONITOR AND CONTROL RISK

The purpose of this task is to monitor and control risk during the project by diligently looking for, identifying and analyzing new risks. This task is ongoing throughout the Project Execution and Control phase.

This task only deals with identifying and analyzing new risks during the project. Once identified, the risks are periodically reviewed using Manage Risk (RKM.040).

APPROACH

Identify Risk and Add to Risk Log

During Project Execution and Control, a risk may arise. This could happen at any time or may be the result of a regular risk assessment. A Risk Form should be completed and the risk should be added to the Risk Log in accordance with the Risk Management Plan component of the Project Management Plan (PMP).

Analyze Each Risk

Analyze each risk for:

  • Criticality– Importance to the Success of the Project (3=High, 2=Medium, 1=Low)
  • Probability – Likelihood of Occurring (3=High, 2=Medium, 1=Low, 0=Closed)
  • Timeframe – Timeframe of the Potential Impact (3=Near, 2=Medium, 1=Far)
  • Severity – Criticality * Probability * Timeframe

Update the Risk Log with the analysis information.

Create a Mitigation Plan for Each Risk

For each risk, create a Mitigation Plan. The Mitigation Plan details a mitigating response for each trigger event. If possible, document that plan directly in the Risk Log. If the Mitigation Plan for a risk requires more detail than can be reasonably documented in the Risk Log, create a separate document.

Now that the risk has been identified and analyzed and a Risk Mitigation Plan has been created, it is periodically reviewed using Manage Risk (RFM.040).
