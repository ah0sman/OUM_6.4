PS.ACT.RPAC - REVIEW PROJECT ASSETS WITH CUSTOMER

This activity finalizes the gap between the Bid Preparation process and Project Start Up.

Back to Top

OBJECTIVES

The objective of this activity is to review the contract and project approach with the customer and confirm there is a common understanding. During this same period, discussions should begin regarding how the project will be governed and controlled. This information will be captured in the Project Management Plan (PMP). The initial PMP will be created during this activity. It will then be updated during the Validate Scope, Stakeholders and OCM Strategy activity and again in the Establish Governance activity. At the end of the Establish Governance activity, the PMP will be finalized.
