PC.ACT.CPC - CLOSE PROCESSES AND CONTRACT

This activity closes out the project processes for each of the project management processes. In addition, any contractual obligations for goods and services are reviewed to make sure that all obligations have been met.

Back to Top

OBJECTIVES

The objective of this activity is to update any final reports and close out each of the project management processes and verify that any contractual obligations for goods and services have been met.

APPROACH

The approach to this activity is to:

  • Close processes and complete any reports for each of the Project Management processes.
  • Submit final reports.
  • Close any open contracts for services and goods.
  • Establish follow-up procedures.
